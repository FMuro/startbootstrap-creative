    About me

    I did my PhD at the Universidad de Sevilla in 2004. Then I spent two years at the Max-Planck-Institut für Mathematik and two and a half years at the Universitat de Barcelona as a postdoc. I moved back to Seville in 2009 with an associate professorship and became a full professor in 2023. Here, I'm a member of the Mathematical Institute and of the Algebra Department.
